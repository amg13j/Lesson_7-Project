What is a Content Delivery Network (CDN)?  List and explain at least FOUR advantages in using a CDN.
A context delivery network is a system of distributed servers(networks) that delivers webpages and other web context to a user based on the geographic locations of the user,the orgin of the webpage and a content delivery server.
Four Advantages include: Higher Capacity Scale, Lower Delivery Costs, Lower Network Latency and Pack loss and Higher Availability.
